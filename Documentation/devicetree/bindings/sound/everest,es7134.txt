ES7134 i2s DA converter

Required properties:
- compatible : "everest,es7134" or
               "everest,es7144" or
	       "everest,es7154"

Optional properties:
- VDD-supply: regulator phandle for the VDD supply
- AVDD-supply: regulator phandle for the AVDD supply

Example:

i2s_codec: external-codec {
	compatible = "everest,es7134";
};
