Spreadtrum SC2731 PMIC battery charger binding

Required properties:
 - compatible: Should be "sprd,sc2731-charger".
 - reg: Address offset of charger register.
 - phys: Contains a phandle to the USB phy.

Example:

	charger@0 {
		compatible = "sprd,sc2731-charger";
		reg = <0x0>;
		phys = <&ssphy>;
	};
